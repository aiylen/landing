Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to install anything?
No installation, no account, and no app store. You'll download a folder — just open index.html in Chrome, Safari, Edge or Firefox and start planning.
Will my data be saved if I close the tab?
Yes — everything auto-saves to your browser's local storage on your own device, so it's there next time you open the same file in the same browser.
Does it work on my phone?
Yes. The layout is fully responsive and adapts to phones, tablets and desktops.
Can I use this for a small business or just myself?
Both! It's built for personal daily planning, but the budget, notes and goal tools work just as well for solo entrepreneurs and freelancers.
Is this a subscription?
No. It's a one-time digital download that's yours to keep and reuse forever.
